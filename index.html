eve parser by brindzal

EVE-Online "market dump" parser, written in python. I'm new to python, mind you.

This small script, lovingly named “trade fucker” (yes, an obvious Penny Arcade reference), parses text files EVE will spit out when you click a button in the bottom center of item price and locations listing screen.
